
Determining the greatest all-around shooter in NBA history is subjective and depends on personal opinions. However, one name that often comes up in this discussion is Stephen Curry. Curry, a guard for the Golden State Warriors, is widely regarded as one of the greatest shooters in NBA history. His exceptional three-point shooting ability, combined with his range, accuracy, and shot creativity, has revolutionized the game.

Other players often mentioned in this conversation include Ray Allen, known for his smooth shooting stroke and clutch performances, and Reggie Miller, a sharpshooter with a prolific career. Ultimately, opinions on the greatest all-around shooter may vary, as it's influenced by factors like playing style, era, and personal preferences.
